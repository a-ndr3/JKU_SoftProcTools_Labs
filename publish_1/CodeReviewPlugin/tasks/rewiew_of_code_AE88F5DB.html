        Main Description

        May contain various things such as: detailed feedback on the code, potential issues/bugs/improvements.

        Steps
        Review the submitted code, paying attention to its correctness, readability, maintainability and other requirements and standards
        Provide detailed feedback on the code, highlighting any areas of improvement/issues/bugs etc.
        *Discuss found bugs/issues if any with other reviwers or developers and document them
        *Review updated code after it's been submitted to ensure that all the found issues from previous steps are covered
